IEEE TechFest 2025

Global Technology Festival | Flagship IEEE Event

You are cordially invited to IEEE TechFest 2025, a premier global initiative establishing one of the largest student-focused IEEE technology events, commencing in Sri Lanka with plans for expansion across the region and beyond.

Organized by the IEEE Sri Lanka Section Student Activities Committee (SLSAC), this distinguished event brings together IEEE Student Branches, Technical Chapters, Women in Engineering Affinity Groups, Universities, and Industry Leaders to advance technology, research, innovation, and professional networking.
